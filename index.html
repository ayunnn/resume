Research Experience

Effect on Osteoinduction of Surface Curvature of Porous Scaffolds
Oct. 2019 - Dec. 2020
Medical Imaging and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory

Developed the bone regeneration model, investigated how the surface curvature of porous scaffolds affect osteoinduction based on in vivo and in silico studies

  • Validated the model using experimental data from rabbits
  • Solved the diffusion equation by Lattice Boltzmann method
  • Developed the model in C++ and accelerated the simulation on the GPU
Computational Simulation of Trabecular Bone Remodeling
Aug. 2020 - Present
Medical Imaging and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory

Proposed an innovative bone prediction algorithm and reproduced the morphological features of actual bone.

  • Coupled the physiological stochasticity in bone remodeling with the conventional topology optimization algorithm
  • Provided a method to predict trabecular structure in diverse physiological situations
Structure Design of Porous Scaffolds
Jul. 2017 - Oct. 2019

Analyzed the bone regeneration process in scaffolds with different structures based on a bone regeneration model to disclose the role of architecture in bone ingrowth

  • Coupled immunoreaction of bone regeneration in the mathematical model
  • Designed a composite structure which increased the percentage of bone deposition at contact surface by 5 percent
Simulation of Foam Metal Formation
Jul. 2017 - Jul. 2018

Investigated the evolution of cell structure in metal foams by numerical simulation using the free surface Lattice Boltzmann method

  • Implement the complex and dynamically moving interfaces between the metal and gas bubble by the Volume of Fluid (VoF) method
  • Improved the algorithm to estimate the curvature of an interface from a digital image
Simulation Study on Bone Ingrowth
Dec. 2016 - May 2017
Outstanding Graduation Project

Proposed a multi-scale bone regeneration model to study the ingrowth process of bone in porous implants

  • Integrated cell activities, growth factors diffusion, angiogenesis and oxygen transportation into the model
  • Applied alternative direction implicit finite-difference method to solve the partial differential equation
